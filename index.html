Our services

Adult Driving Programs Interested in earning your driver’s license? National Driving School has over a decade of experience teaching Las Vegas-area residents how to drive in Nevada. Our friendly customer service staff will work with you to schedule behind-the-wheel driving lessons with our licensed, trained driving instructors at times that work within your busy schedule. National Driving School also offers a Rules of the Road review class to give you the background necessary to earn your instruction permit. Learn to drive with National Driving School!

Requirements for Signing Up for Adult Programs:

18 years old minimum.

2. Either: a valid Nevada driver’s license, a valid international driver’s license, or a valid instruction permit issued by the Nevada DMV.
Teen Driving Programs Earning a driver’s license has long been a rite of passage for teens. National   Driving School is pleased to offer both classroom instruction and behind-the-wheel driving lessons to teens throughout the Southern Nevada .

In order to obtain his/her license, the state requires every teen to have 30 hours in-class instruction with 8 hours behind-the-wheel, or 30 hours of in-class instruction with 6 hours behind-the-wheel and 6 hours of observation.

All of our Drivers Education Teen Programs include the complimentary National Safety Council Defensive Driving Course “Alive at 25″. Our teens will be receiving their NSC certificates in addition to National Driving School’s Certificate. Have Questions? call (702) 596-5437 for more details.
Defensive Driving Courses

Driving School is proud to be among the handful of providers authorized to teach the National Safety Council’s acclaimed Defensive Driving Courses.We are a National Safety Council certified training center with the ability to offer: Alive at 25, DDC4, DDC6, and DDC8

Corporations that offer their employees any of the defensive courses available stand to gain more at the end of the day since they are likely to:

  • Create a culture of safety.
  • Lower workers’ compensation insurance cost.
  • Decrease insurance premiums on company vehicles.
  • Reduce liability exposure.
